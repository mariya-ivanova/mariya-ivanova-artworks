Welcome to my web page

I was born on 11.11.1977 ... and I'm quite an introverted person, probably because I've been through a lot in my personal life. So I don't have social media accounts so far.
I have changed a few different professions during the years, I have interests in various professional fields, and painting, design and photography are just some of them.
I like to paint since I was a child, but I have decided to return to art and design just recently. I believe it is never too late. And I also plan to share my life experience with some text designs in the future.
